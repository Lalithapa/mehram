ETHENIC WEAR: AN ESSENTIAL FOR ALL FESTIVE EVENTS

Nothing is more powerful than an ethnic dress stitched from the finest fabrics. When we talk about ethnic dresses, we think of plush fabrics, fine weaving, rich colours and eye-catching prints. Thus, Libas’ fashion experts have put their heads together to design ethnic wear for women that represents the mindset of the stylish and modern Indian woman.

Stepping out in ethnic women's clothing from Libas sets a new bar for fashion lovers. Each ethnic piece has been designed to prioritise comfort without compromising on style. We launch a new selection every fifteen days to keep you up to date with the latest and fashionable ethnic styles. Our popular collections include Leher, Saanjh, Bahaar, Ajrakh, Rooh, Nazm,Nazaaqat, Gul, The Indigo Story and Noor. Each collection features beautiful details like embroidery, Gota Patti, Khari Print, Ajrakh, Schiffli, and many more fascinating embellishments.


STEP INTO THE WORLD OF WOMEN'S ETHNIC WEAR WITH LIBAS

SUITS:

Suits are a well-coordinated ethnic set that makes you appear elegant and smart. Women's suits feature different types of kurtas such as Anarkali, A-line, straight-fit and more. In addition, you can shop from a variety of bottoms such as palazzos, churidars, salwars, and cigarette pants.


KURTAS:

Ladies’ Kurtis and kurtas are available in different sleeve lengths, cuts, prints and patterns. Select from a variety of pieces featuring ; different types of necklines, sleeves, hemlines, and more that allow you to experiment with unique silhouettes. |


PALAZZO:

We bring you timeless drapes in a variety of prints and embellishments. Take your style a notch higher with silk, chiffon and cotton palazzo's. These fabrics exude culture, grace, and sophistication unlike any other.


DRESSES:

Libas Extra Love range is dedicated to offering stylish plus-size ethnic wear options featuring our signature designs. Take your pick from a variety of different cuts, fits, colours and prints.
